GUIDELINES TO APPLICANTS

The workshop invites interested researchers in Cloud Computing & Web Services to submit the application online with their research contribution that includes a scanned copy of the attested list of their publications/presentations (if any) and/or scanned copy of their attested unpublished research paper, (if any) in IEEE format on any of the below listed topics.

Topics of interest
  • Applications, Protocols, Networks and Architectures
  • Performance Modeling, Simulation and Prediction
  • Networking and Cloud Computing Infrastructure
  • Fault Tolerance, Reliability and Availability
  • Security, Configuration, Policy Issues and Challenges
  • Management, Monitoring, Scheduling and Load-Balancing
  • Virtualization
  • Mobile Intelligent Services
  • Data Storage
  • Web Services
  • Computational Intelligence

As the workshop limits number of participants, the applicants with research contibution would be given preference for participation.
